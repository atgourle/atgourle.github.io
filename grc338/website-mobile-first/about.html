About

It all starts with Ryan Sherwood

Decades ago, Ryan Sherwood's bold vision and recipe for success laid the foundation for one of the world’s most legendary steak houses.

From Humble Beginnings

In 1965 our founder Ryan Sherwood risked it all and mortgaged his home to buy a small steak house in Laguna Beach. As a college dropout, Sherwood taught himself the restaurant business, endured countless challenges and, in the process, redefined hospitality.

Our Values

We are committed to: Creating “Guests for Life” that are loyal to the Sherwood brand. Providing a positive, safe and supportive work environment in which hospitality-minded employees are developed, trained and rewarded.Continuous improvement and company growth. Fostering lasting relationships with vendors, business associates, and communities where we do business.

Our Mission

At Sherwood Steakhouse, we aspire to create the finest experience for our guests by combining the highest quality steaks and seafood, with exceptional service in a cosmopolitan and entertaining atmosphere.
